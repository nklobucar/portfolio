England

From Tower Hamlets to Bournemouth

Located in East London just north of the Thames, Tower Hamlets blends modern urban living with historical significance, creating a dynamic and culturally rich area.
A 3.5 hour train journey southwest of London lies Bovington, which hosts the largest tank museum in Europe. Coincidentally, I arrived on the weekend of their annual festival, Tankfest.
Standing tall above the English Channel lies Dover, a port town with uniqely bright cliffs. Created from millions of years of limestone formation from the sea floor, exposed after sea levels receded.
